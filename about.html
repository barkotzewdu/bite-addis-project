Our Story

Welcome to BITE ADDIS. Located in the heart of Addis Ababa, we believe that fast food should never compromise on quality.

Started in 2023, our mission was simple: to serve the crispiest fries, the juiciest burgers, and the most refreshing shakes in town using locally sourced ingredients.

Whether you are grabbing a quick lunch or hanging out with friends, our modern, clean, and vibrant space is designed for you.
